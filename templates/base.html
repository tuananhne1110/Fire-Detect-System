About Us

Welcome to our deep learning project dedicated to enhancing fire detection in real-time using cameras. We are a team of passionate and driven university students, coming together to make a meaningful impact on fire safety. Our project is a manifestation of our commitment to leveraging cutting-edge technology to improve safety measures in a variety of environments.

Our Mission

In a world where fire incidents can have devastating consequences, our mission is to create a system that empowers individuals and organizations to detect fires as early as possible. By utilizing camera-based fire detection, we aim to offer a wide-ranging solution suitable for large spaces, warehouses, and any area where traditional smoke detection systems might fall short. Our primary focus is on ensuring the safety and well-being of people and their assets, and we believe that early fire detection is an essential step in achieving this goal.
